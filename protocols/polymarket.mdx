## Overview

Polymarket is one of the world's best prediction markets, allowing you to stay informed and profit from your knowledge by betting on future events across various topics.

Apart from predictions, it can be used as a perfect hedging venue for your correlated perp and options positions.

## Why Polymarket?

Polymarket provides a unique venue for:

- **Hedging correlated positions** - Offset risk from your crypto derivatives
- **Event-driven trading** - Capitalize on real-world events
- **Market sentiment** - Gauge crowd wisdom on future outcomes
- **Portfolio diversification** - Add uncorrelated strategies to your portfolio
