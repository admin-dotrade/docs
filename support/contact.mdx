## Common Issues

### Bot Not Responding

1. Make sure you've started the bot with `/start`
2. Check if you've set your exchange with `/setexchange`
3. Verify your wallet has sufficient balance

### Bridge Issues

1. Ensure you have USDC in your wallet
2. Check that you've approved the bridge transaction
3. Wait for blockchain confirmation (may take a few minutes)

### Trading Errors

1. Verify you have sufficient balance on the exchange
2. Check if the market is active
3. Ensure your order size meets minimum requirements

<Note>
  If you continue to experience issues, please reach out to our support team on
  Telegram.
</Note>

## Report a Bug

Found a bug? Please report it through:

1. **Telegram**: Message our support team with details
2. **GitHub**: Open an issue in our repository (if public)

When reporting a bug, please include:

- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Expected vs actual behavior
- Screenshots (if applicable)
- Your username (for context)
